Frequently Asked Questions

What is SafeBites?
SafeBites is a platform that helps people with food allergies find allergy-friendly restaurants.
How do I search for restaurants?
You can search for restaurants by entering your specific allergies in the search bar.
Can I leave reviews?
Yes! Users can leave reviews based on their dining experiences at various restaurants.
Is there an app for SafeBites?
Currently, SafeBites is a web-based platform, but we are working on an app version!
