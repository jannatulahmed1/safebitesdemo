FAQs

What is SafeBites?
SafeBites is a platform designed to help individuals with food allergies and dietary restrictions find safe dining options.
How do I search for allergy-friendly restaurants?
Simply enter your allergy in the search bar, and SafeBites will provide a list of compatible restaurants.
How can I share my experience at a restaurant?
You can contribute by leaving reviews and ratings to guide others in finding allergy-safe dining options.
